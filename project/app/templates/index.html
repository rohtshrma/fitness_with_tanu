Program & Journey: Our 4-Steps Process Worth The Weight

Pre-Consultation Stage

You fill the ‘Let us know you’ form to help us figure out your existing eating pattern and workout routine, what are your expectations from the program and health parameters which are important to you

CONSULTATION STAGE

Online Consultation basis the information set and discussion to know each other better. We will start our first meeting where we work together on devising an eating pattern that is sustainable and keeps you fit. Your interest areas, preferences and final agreed plan will be devised

Weekly Interaction

Weekly interactions shall be planned to review and alter your meal and exercise plans.We will review the progress and we are flexible for all medium to touch base upon you Whatsapp Call, Email, Whatsapp, Zoom meeting etc

POST-CONSULTATION STAGE

Your journey completion document and your to do to help you maintain the health and nutrition.

About

Find Out More About Tanu

With the interest and intent to spread awareness Tanu has been contributing her bit by adding value to people’s life. Her life’s motto has been helping people around to take best decision of investing in their Nutrition and health and to live & lead a better healthy life. Tanu has been meritorious throughout her education and recipient of University Gold Medal. After her post-graduation she joined Fortis Escort Heart Institute (FEHI), New Delhi. After working for 8 years she joined as Head of Nutrition in Aakash Healthcare and has played pivotal role in the journey of the establishment of the Hospital in the early years and getting the hospital NABH accreditation. Tanu has expertise in the field of Nutrition & Diet therapy . She wisely deal with the therapeutic diets for cardiac, diabetic, renal and gastro patients. She actively Participates in the Lifestyle Management programme for Weight Reduction under medical supervision. She is always ready to contribute in the developmental work of the Nutritional aspects of various research(es).
