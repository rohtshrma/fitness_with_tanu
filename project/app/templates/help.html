Program & Journey: Our 4-Steps Process Worth The Weight

Pre-Consultation Stage

You fill the ‘Let us know you’ form to help us figure out your existing eating pattern and workout routine, what are your expectations from the program and health parameters which are important to you

CONSULTATION STAGE

Online Consultation basis the information set and discussion to know each other better. We will start our first meeting where we work together on devising an eating pattern that is sustainable and keeps you fit. Your interest areas, preferences and final agreed plan will be devised

Weekly Interaction

Weekly interactions shall be planned to review and alter your meal and exercise plans.We will review the progress and we are flexible for all medium to touch base upon you Phone Call, Email, Whatsapp, Zoom meeting etc

POST-CONSULTATION STAGE

Your journey completion document and your to do to help you maintain the health and nutrition.
